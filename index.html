what kind of Project?

Outlaster is a 2d Microgame zombie shooter made in Unity. The game is inspired by multiple 2d topdown games and with a zombie shooter aspect the Project lasted around 4 weeks and was made in a team of 3 people. it was challanging using new techniques and learning how to work with a team but overall it was a great experience and I learned a lot about game development and teamwork.
